Charter-specific Scope 3 emissions factor could raise reported tonnage 18–32% on art-tour movements

ICCT's 19 May working paper recommends replacing the IATA fleet-average emissions factor with a charter-specific factor that accounts for load factor below 60%. Most art and live-events charter movements load at 35–55%. CSRD-reporting workspaces would see Scope 3 figures rise materially next reporting cycle.
